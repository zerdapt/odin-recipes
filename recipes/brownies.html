Best Homemade Brownies

Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 40–48 minutes | Serves: 16 brownies

Equipment

  • 8×8 baking dish
  • Cooking spray
  • Parchment paper

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup cocoa powder, sifted if lumpy
  • ½ cup powdered sugar, sifted if lumpy
  • ½ cup dark chocolate chips
  • ¾ teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup canola oil or extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Spray and line an 8×8 pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle dry mixture over wet and gently fold until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth the top.
  6. Bake 40–48 minutes, until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  7. Let cool completely before slicing. Store at room temperature up to 3 days (or freeze up to a month).

Notes

  • You can reduce sugar to 1 cup granulated if desired.
  • Olive oil adds flavor; use canola for neutral taste.
  • Pull brownies slightly underbaked—they’ll firm up as they cool.
